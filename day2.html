🌟 HTML

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the foundation of the web. It tells the browser what content to display (text, images, videos, links, etc.) and how the content is structured.

Think of HTML as the skeleton 🦴 of a webpage. CSS is the skin & design 💅, and JavaScript is the brain 🧠 & interactivity.

The Purpose: To structure your document in a way understandable to both humans and machines 🤖. Not just for making text big; it's for structural importance.

📝 Paragraph

A paragraph in HTML is defined using the <p> tag. It groups sentences into meaningful blocks of text.

Browsers automatically add spacing before and after each paragraph, making content visually clear.

Paragraphs help in organizing content logically, ensuring a better reading experience for users.

They are fundamental tags used in almost every webpage 🌍.

➖ Horizontal Ruler

The <hr> tag creates a thematic break or division in content.

It is self-closing, meaning it does not need a closing tag.

Browsers render it as a horizontal line across the width of its container.

📋 Lists

Unordered List
  • Tea 🍵
  • Sugar 🍬
  • Milk 🥛
Unordered lists are used when order does not matter. Markers: disc (●), circle (○), square (■).
Ordered List
  1. Boil water 🔥
  2. Add tea leaves 🍃
  3. Add sugar and milk 🥛🍬
  4. Serve hot ☕
Ordered lists are used when the order matters.
